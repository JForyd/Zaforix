Our Story

About

Why We Exist

Who We Are

Kozanni is an American sports car and transportation technology company. We design and build world-class performance vehicles and mobility technologies.

Our company's culture of improvement is what unites us in our pursuit for working through the most complex and challenging problems of our day.

Why We Do What We Do

For us, the reward of our work is the process of building something better. Solving problems, refining ideas, and collating with others to improve machines one step at a time.

It is those we serve that continute to motivate us to do better, reach higher, and work harder than ever before. The goal has never been about perfection, but stead improvement with every iteration brings us one more step towards our potential.

Core Values

◈

Uncompromised Design

Every vehicle is a sculpture. We refuse to compromise on aesthetic integrity, creating forms that are as emotionally compelling as they are aerodynamically efficient.

⬡

Engineering Excellence

Precision engineering is our foundation. From powertrain architecture to suspension geometry, every system is developed to deliver an unparalleled driving experience.

◉

Relentless Innovation

We challenge convention at every turn, integrating advanced materials, computational design, and next-generation technologies to redefine performance standards.

Proud To Be American

Owned and Operated In The United States of America

Kozanni is proud to be an American company. Our work is shaped by a long tradition of builders, engineers, and innovators who believed that progress comes from hard work, curiosity, and the willingness to try something new.

From engineers and designers to machinists, fabricators, technicians, and scientists, the country as we know it today came to be from the hardworking hands of craftsmen. That pride in our work and love for country continues to shape the culture at Kozanni.
