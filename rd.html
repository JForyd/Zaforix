Current Phase

Zaforix is currently in an intensive research and development phase. Our multidisciplinary team is exploring groundbreaking concepts across vehicle design, powertrain engineering, aerodynamics, and advanced materials — laying the foundation for a new era of high-performance machines.

From computational fluid dynamics to next-generation propulsion architecture, every avenue of innovation is being pursued with singular focus: to deliver a driving experience that sets an entirely new benchmark.

Areas of Exploration

Aerodynamics

Advanced CFD simulations and wind tunnel testing drive our pursuit of optimal drag coefficients and downforce balance at extreme velocities.

Exploring high-performance electric and hybrid powertrains delivering instantaneous torque with unprecedented efficiency and range.
